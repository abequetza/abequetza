## About

I am a cybersecurity and software engineering profile focused on building secure, reliable, and production-ready systems. My work sits at the intersection of secure infrastructure, full-stack development, AI-assisted engineering, automation, SIEM detection, identity, cloud tooling, and product-focused delivery.

I approach engineering with a security-first mindset: clean architecture, resilient authentication flows, hardened containers, documented deployment pipelines, measurable operational quality, and systems that remain maintainable beyond the first release.

My current focus is on designing full-stack platforms, strengthening infrastructure security, applying AI and LLMs to engineering workflows, and building tools that improve visibility, automation, and decision-making for technical teams.
